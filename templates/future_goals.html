Future Goals

Our roadmap for transforming India's political landscape through next-generation leadership

Building For Bharat's Future

Building Bharat is committed to long-term, systemic change in India's political ecosystem. Our future goals represent our ambitious vision for developing ethical, effective political leadership at scale across the nation.

Our Development Timeline

2023-2024

Foundation Phase

  • Establish core training curriculum
  • Expand to 10 university campuses
  • Conduct pilot programs in 5 states
  • Launch the Building Bharat Fellowship (Beta)

2024-2025

Growth Phase

  • Scale to 25 campuses nationwide
  • Full launch of Building Bharat Fellowship
  • Develop and deploy AI CRM Tool for political offices
  • Pilot Volunteer Exchange Program

2025-2026

Consolidation Phase

  • Establish regional hubs in 5 metro cities
  • Launch Political Leadership Certification program
  • Expand Volunteer Exchange to nationwide network
  • Create policy incubator for youth-led initiatives

2026-2027

Impact & Scale Phase

  • Support 100+ candidates in local/state elections
  • Reach 1000+ political leaders through training
  • Launch governance monitoring platform
  • Develop international partnerships

Flagship Future Initiatives

Building Bharat Fellowship

A prestigious year-long program that will select and nurture 20 exceptional young political talents annually, providing:

  • Comprehensive political leadership training
  • Mentorship from experienced political leaders
  • Field placements with elected representatives
  • Seed funding for community initiatives
  • Policy research opportunities

Target Launch: July 2024

Volunteer Exchange Program

A nationwide network connecting political campaigns with trained volunteers across India, featuring:

  • Skill-based volunteer matching platform
  • Cross-regional campaign experience opportunities
  • Standardized volunteer training curriculum
  • Campaign best practices repository
  • Impact measurement framework

Target Launch: December 2024

AI CRM Tool for Political Offices

An innovative constituent relationship management system designed specifically for Indian political contexts:

  • Multilingual constituent engagement tracking
  • Issue categorization and pattern identification
  • Resource allocation optimization
  • Development project monitoring
  • Performance dashboards for representatives

Target Launch: March 2025

Research & Knowledge Creation

Building Bharat Policy Journal

A peer-reviewed publication showcasing research and thought leadership on political innovation, governance, and leadership development in the Indian context.

The journal will feature:

  • Research from emerging political leaders
  • Case studies of political innovation
  • Governance best practices
  • Leadership development frameworks

Target Launch: January 2025

Indian Political Leadership Index

An annual assessment of political leadership development across India, providing data-driven insights into the state of leadership capacity, innovation, and effectiveness.

The index will measure:

  • Youth participation in political structures
  • Leadership diversity and inclusion
  • Political innovation adoption
  • Governance performance metrics
  • Political ethics and accountability

Target Launch: October 2025
