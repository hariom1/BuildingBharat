Our Vision

Building a new generation of ethical, effective political leadership for Bharat

Our Vision for India's Political Future

We envision an India where young, capable, and ethical leaders drive political discourse and governance, bringing innovation, integrity, and inclusivity to public service.

A New Political Paradigm

Building Bharat aims to create a fundamental shift in Indian politics - from personality-driven to policy-driven, from dynasty-oriented to merit-based, and from reactive to proactive governance.

The Three Pillars of Our Vision

Youth-Led Innovation

Empowering young leaders with modern tools, technological literacy, and innovative approaches to governance challenges

Ethical Leadership

Developing leaders committed to transparency, accountability, and integrity in public service and political discourse

Evidence-Based Governance

Promoting data-driven policy-making, outcomes-based evaluation, and continuous learning in governance

Our Origin Story

Building Bharat emerged from the Public Policy & Governance Society (PPGS) at IIT Kharagpur, where a group of passionate students and faculty members recognized a critical gap in India's political ecosystem: the lack of structured training and support for young political aspirants.

What began as campus workshops and policy simulations in 2018 has now evolved into Building Bharat - a comprehensive platform that bridges academia, politics, and governance to nurture the next generation of India's political leadership.

Our founding team combines expertise from political science, public policy, technology, and campaign management to create a holistic approach to political leadership development.
