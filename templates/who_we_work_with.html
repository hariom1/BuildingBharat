Vikram Singh

Student Body President to Zila Panchayat Member

After participating in our Political Leadership Bootcamp, Vikram leveraged his campus leadership experience to win a seat in his district's Zila Panchayat, focusing on education reform and youth employment.

"Building Bharat helped me translate academic ideas into practical governance initiatives that resonated with voters in my rural constituency."

Priya Sharma

Policy Researcher to Youth Wing Leader

Priya transitioned from academic research to a leadership position in her party's youth wing after attending our Campaign Strategy Workshop, where she developed skills in digital campaigning and volunteer management.

"The evidence-based approach to political messaging taught by Building Bharat transformed how our youth wing communicates policy positions."

Community Organizer to MLA's Chief of Staff

After completing our Political Assistant Training, Rajesh applied his new skills in constituency management and stakeholder engagement to become an effective chief of staff for a first-time MLA.
