Frequently Asked Questions

You can join Building Bharat by filling out the contact form above, indicating your interest in our leadership development programs. Our team will reach out to you with information about upcoming programs, eligibility criteria, and application processes.

No prior political experience is required. Building Bharat programs are designed for both first-time political aspirants and those with some experience. Our focus is on developing your leadership potential and providing you with the skills and knowledge needed for effective political engagement.

Building Bharat is a non-partisan organization. Our programs focus on developing leadership skills, ethical governance practices, and effective campaign strategies that can be applied across political ideologies. We welcome participants from all democratic political backgrounds.

We welcome partnerships with academic institutions, civil society organizations, think tanks, and other entities aligned with our mission.
